The aim of the Chinese Communication Association is to promote, enhance, and facilitate scholarly activities and exchanges on Chinese communication, which is broadly defined to embrace any aspect of communication studies concerning mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan.

For the last twenty years, CCA has been home to Chinese communication scholars and those interested in Chinese communication around the world. At this time of technological advances when timely information is truly valued, our collective work to extend understanding of communication has never been more important.
