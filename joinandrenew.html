Benefits of Joining CCA

Opportunities to participate and showcase your research at dedicated CCA panels in major international conventions. CCA pays for panels at the annual conferences of the National Communication Association (NCA), the International Communication Association (ICA), and the Association for Education in Journalism and Mass Communication (AEJMC).

Special Access to the CCA social gatherings at the major conferences mentioned above. These are large social network opportunities and are attended by hundreds of scholars.

A showcase to promote your own research and teaching achievements.

Job announcements that specifically target Chinese communication scholars. In addition to the newsletter, the CCA announces job ads through its email listserv, and social media outlets. Each year we receive job ads from universities in Australia, China, Singapore, Taiwan, and other countries that seeking scholars who specialize in Chinese communication.

Subscription to CCA newsletters, two issues per year.

Free access to Communication & Society (傳播與社會學刊). CCA members will get all its articles in PDF files.

Most of all, CCA provides you a home away from home at large international conventions.
